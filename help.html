How to Use This Website

  1. Location: Enter a city name (e.g., "Paris") or latitude and longitude coordinates (e.g., "48.8566,2.3522"). Alternatively, select a continent to search top cities there.
  2. Dates: Choose your trip start and end dates. The maximum trip length is 16 days due to API limits.
  3. Temperature Preferences: Optionally enter your preferred minimum and maximum temperatures in Fahrenheit.
  4. Rain Preference: Check the box if you want to exclude destinations with any rain during your trip.
  5. Submit: Click "Find Destinations" to get the best matching places based on your inputs.

The results will show average temperature, precipitation, and a score based on how well the destination matches your preferences.
