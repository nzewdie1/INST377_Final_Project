What is this?

This website helps you plan your next trip based on your weather preferences, location, and date! You can choose specific cities or search across continents. The site uses live weather data from Open-Meteo and visualizes temperature trends using Chart.js.

How to use it

  1. Go to the Planner page.
  2. Select a date range, your desired weather range, and location preference (city or continent).
  3. See a ranked list of recommended places to visit.
  4. Save your favorite searches and view the weather trends!
